My Interests

Web Development

I have a deep interest in the world of web development, especially in the process of bringing a design to life as an interactive interface. For me, it's a truly enjoyable challenge to make an application feel responsive and easy to use on any device. I love experimenting with modern frameworks and technologies because there's always something new to learn and a better way to build things. Seeing lines of code transform into a seamless user experience is incredibly rewarding.

Artificial Intelligence

I have a great interest in the mechanics behind Artificial Intelligence. The idea that a machine can "learn" from data to recognize patterns, make decisions, or even create is absolutely fascinating to me. I enjoy reading and learning about different AI model architectures and the logic behind them. The process of training a model and watching it gradually become "smarter" is something that sparks my curiosity on both a technical and philosophical level..

Cybersecurity

To me, cybersecurity is a dynamic, never-ending puzzle. I'm fascinated by the cat-and-mouse game between attackers and defenders. Learning about attack methodologies and thinking like an ethical hacker to find vulnerabilities is a deeply satisfying intellectual challenge. It's not about breaking things, but about understanding a system's weaknesses in order to build more resilient and robust defenses.

Gaming

For me, gaming is an arena for honing skills, strategy, and teamwork. I enjoy the challenge and adrenaline rush of competitive games. The process of mastering game mechanics, communicating effectively with a team, and achieving a common goal provides a unique sense of satisfaction. It's a great way to practice staying calm under pressure and problem-solving on the fly.
